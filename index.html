The Computer Science Club of CUNY Queens College

About Us

Code ReSolve is a student organization at Queens College that aims to provide a space for students to learn about computer science and technology. We host workshops, events, and other activities to help students learn about computer science and technology. We also host events to help students network with professionals in the field. We are a diverse group of students from all majors and backgrounds. We welcome all students to join us!
